Shipping, Returns & Customer Support – Jawaxa Ethnics

How long does delivery take?

We usually dispatch your order within 24 to 48 hours.

Delivery time after dispatch is typically 2 to 7 business days, depending on your location.

We only work with reputed courier partners to ensure timely and safe delivery.

What if my order is delayed?

In rare cases, delivery may be delayed due to courier issues, festivals, or bad weather.

We will keep you informed and assist you with tracking updates at any time.

Is delivery free?

Yes, we offer free delivery on all prepaid orders above ₹2,499.

For orders below ₹2,499, a ₹50 delivery fee is charged.

What is a prepaid order?

A prepaid order is when you pay online before the order is shipped.

It is secure, convenient, and qualifies for free delivery if your order is above ₹2,499.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?

Yes, COD is available for orders up to ₹2,000.

An additional ₹100 COD handling fee will apply.

Why is WhatsApp confirmation required for COD?

To prevent fake or incomplete orders, we confirm every COD order on WhatsApp before dispatch.

Orders without confirmation will not be processed.

What if I paid by COD and the product is defective?

In case of a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery.

Once we verify the issue through photo or video proof, we will arrange a free pickup.

Since we currently do not have an e-commerce store or wallet system, your refund will be processed through UPI or direct bank transfer after we receive the returned product.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we accept international orders.

Please contact us via WhatsApp or Email to know the shipping charges, estimated delivery time, and available payment options for your country.

Can I return my saree if I don’t like it?

We accept returns only if the product is damaged or if you received the wrong item.

We do not accept returns for reasons like personal preference or minor color variations.

Which products are not eligible for return?

  • Used, worn, or washed sarees
  • Sarees with slight color variations due to screen or lighting
  • Custom-made or altered sarees

How do I request a return or exchange?

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Ensure the product is unused, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Send clear photo or video proof on WhatsApp or Email
  • Once approved, we will schedule a return pickup

Will you charge for the return pickup?

If the mistake is from our side (damaged or wrong item), we will arrange a return pickup at no extra cost to you.

What is a custom-made saree?

Any saree that has been specially altered, stitched, or designed as per your request is considered custom-made.

Custom-made sarees are not eligible for return or exchange.

Will I receive a bill with my order?

We provide a digital invoice only, sent to you on WhatsApp after order confirmation.

We currently do not provide printed invoices, as we avoid paper usage.
